Lock In Rules
Phone Discipline
Instagram max 30 min/day. No reels during study blocks. Phone in other room while studying. Wind down only after all study is done.
Morning = Brain Time
First thing after waking is water, not phone. Morning blocks are sacred — no compromises. Your brain is sharpest then, don't waste it.
Run is Non-Negotiable
Start at whatever distance you can. Build to 5km over 2-3 months. Missing run = missing the glowup. Body and mind are connected.
Green / Yellow / Red Days
Green = full schedule hit. Yellow = 70%+ done. Red = slacked. Track it. 2 reds in a row = mandatory accountability check.
DPPs Same Day
Aakash DPPs solved the same day as the class. Never let them pile up. Backlog is where motivation goes to die.
Disappear Mode
Next Instagram post = AIIMS entrance. Nothing in between. The world doesn't need to know what you're doing. Let the results speak.
